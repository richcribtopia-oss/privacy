Cribtopia LLC is the developer and publisher of this application. We are a real estate technology company based in the United States. Our games are standalone entertainment and utility products brought to you by the Cribtopia brand.

For questions about this policy, please contact us using the Support link at the bottom of this page.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our games are designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers

3. Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Like most apps distributed through the Apple App Store, certain non-personal technical data may be collected automatically by Apple or third-party analytics services integrated into the platform. This may include:

This data is anonymized and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify you personally and is used solely to improve app performance and stability.

Our apps are distributed exclusively through the Apple App Store. Apple's own privacy practices govern any data collected during download, purchase, or use of App Store services. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, social media SDKs, or data brokers into our applications.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately via the Support link below and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

Our games comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps available to younger audiences.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data through our apps, there is no personal data stored on our servers to protect. Any data processed on-device (such as your calculation history in CribCrunch or your game progress in Werdy) remains solely on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Some of our apps may offer one-time premium purchases. All payment transactions are processed exclusively through Apple's in-app purchase system. Cribtopia LLC never receives, stores, or has access to your payment card information. All billing inquiries should be directed to Apple Support.

Our apps may contain links to cribtopia.com or other Cribtopia-branded resources. Once you leave our app and visit an external website, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
